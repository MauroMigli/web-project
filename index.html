What is Lin Go!?

Lin Go! is a language learning app that helps you learn a new language in a fun and easy way. It is an innovative and science based app that harnesses the power of technology to learn.

Lin Go! is based on the latest research in the field of language learning. It uses the latest technology to help you learn a new language in a fun and easy way.
